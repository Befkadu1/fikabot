Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 May 2026

This policy explains how FikaBot AB ("we", "us") handles personal data in connection with the website fikabot.se. We keep data collection to an absolute minimum.

FikaBot AB — Precision Coffee Co.

FikaBot AB is the data controller for the processing described below.

2. What this website does not do

  • It sets no cookies and uses no local or session storage.
  • It uses no analytics, advertising or tracking of any kind.
  • It contains no forms — we never ask you to submit data through the site.
  • Fonts and all assets are served from our own domain, so no data is sent to third-party content networks when you browse.

Because of this, no consent banner is required to visit the site.

3. Data we do process

a) Server logs. Like all websites, our hosting provider records standard technical information when a page is requested — including your IP address, the time of the request, and your browser type. This is used only to operate and secure the site. Legal basis: our legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) in running a safe, functioning website.

b) Correspondence. If you email or call us using the contact details above, we process the information you choose to share (such as your name, contact details and the content of your message) in order to respond and, where relevant, to discuss hosting a FikaBot. Legal basis: steps taken at your request / our legitimate interest in replying (Art. 6(1)(b)–(f) GDPR).

4. Who we share data with

  • Hosting: the website and server logs are hosted by Loopia AB (Sweden, EU).
  • Email: messages you send are received via our email provider's mail servers.

We do not sell your data and do not share it with anyone else for marketing purposes.

5. How long we keep it

Server logs are retained by our host for a limited period for security and troubleshooting. Email correspondence is kept only as long as needed to handle your enquiry and any resulting business relationship, after which it is deleted.

6. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict or object to the processing of your personal data, and the right to data portability. To exercise any of these, contact us at stessema@fikabot.se.

7. Complaints

If you believe we have handled your personal data improperly, you may lodge a complaint with the Swedish Authority for Privacy Protection (IMY — Integritetsskyddsmyndigheten), www.imy.se.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date above always reflects the current version.
